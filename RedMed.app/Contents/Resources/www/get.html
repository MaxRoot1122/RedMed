Set up your NFC medical bracelet.
Only you need the app — a passive chip on the band stores your card (no battery, no broadcast).

Detecting your phone…

Get the app Alternate store Set up in browser
  1. 1Install RedMed (this page sends you to the right store for your phone).
  2. 2Fill your profile on My ID and tap Bracelet (top right).
  3. 3Hold your band to the phone once to program the chip. After that, the passive chip stores your data until a smartphone taps to read it (not card readers or other NFC devices).

The bracelet uses a passive NFC chip (no battery, no broadcast). A smartphone powers it briefly on tap to read your emergency card. RedMed only programs the chip; it cannot install phone firmware.
