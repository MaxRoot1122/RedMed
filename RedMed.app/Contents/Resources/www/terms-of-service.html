What RedMed is

RedMed has no servers. The app is for setting up your NFC medical bracelet — you enter your profile on your phone, then program the band. After setup, strangers tap the chip; they do not need the app. Optional Find 911 and Basic Aid screens run on your device only while the app is open.

Using the app

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you understand that your medical profile stays on your device and on your bracelet chip. RedMed LLC does not collect or host that data. Find 911 may use GPS and compass only while that screen is visible. See the Privacy Policy. The web version shows a one-time notice on first open.

Location (Find 911)

The App may request access to your device's location on the Find 911 screen only for the purpose of showing your coordinates during an emergency — for example, so you can tell a 911 dispatcher where you are or copy them into the on-screen summary. This is not advertising tracking, not background monitoring, and not used for any purpose other than emergency location display on that screen.

RedMed has no servers. Location is processed on your device only. We do not receive, log, or retain your coordinates. Location collection stops when you leave Find 911 or close the App.
