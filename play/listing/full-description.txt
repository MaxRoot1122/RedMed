RedMed — local-first emergency medical ID for NFC bracelets.

Fill in allergies, medications, conditions, and emergency contacts once. Save your profile and write it to an NTAG NFC bracelet. Anyone who taps the band sees a high-contrast emergency card in Safari, Chrome, or any browser — no app install required.

KEY FEATURES
• My ID — edit and save your medical profile on your phone
• NFC Write (iOS) — write your HTTPS card link to a blank bracelet chip
• Emergency card — high-contrast responder view from the tag itself
• Find 911 — GPS coordinates and compass while that screen is open (on-device only)
• Roadside Aid — offline first-aid reference and CPR timer

PRIVACY
• No accounts, no cloud sync of your medical profile
• Data on the tag is intentionally not encrypted so responders can read it
• Location is used only on the Find 911 screen and is not sent to our servers

IMPORTANT
RedMed is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always call 911 (or your local emergency number) in a life-threatening emergency. Information you enter is not verified by RedMed.

Works with NTAG215/216 NFC wristbands, wallet cards, and keychain tags.
